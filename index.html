Reuse, made simple


THE DATA-POWERED REUSABLE PACKAGING TRACKING PLATFORM


Ecotrace's user-friendly software assists companies in establishing and expanding reusable packaging systems that attract customers, enhance ESG outcomes, and produce a return on investment.

Ecotrace was founded to make reuse more common than single use

Dr. Donatus Njoroge set out to discover the reasons why companies weren't recycling their packaging in 2023.

It was time to use what he had learned about leveraging data to overcome Operational and Commercial challenges to the escalating pollution crisis after obtaining a ton of expertise in technology businesses (from start-ups to corporates).

He talked to hundreds of businesses to ascertain their reasons for not recycling their packaging. He discovered individuals who shared his interests: workers in multinational organizations who were aware of consumer demands and the growing threat posed by climate change; individuals who were attempting to implement more sustainable practices but were encountering obstacle after obstacle. Those difficulties were the beginning of transformation, according to Dr. Donatus Njoroge.

In the past 14 weeks, the Ecotrace team has launched 10 reuse systems; we are masters in setting you up from start or integrating with your current setup.

By attaching a machine-readable tag to your B2B or B2C packaging—a QR Code, NFC, RFID, Barcode, or Digital Watermark—we can assist you in making it smarter. We scan this tag to build a digital twin in our system, enabling us to give sophisticated tracking information to drive your scalable, safe reuse solutions.

Your clients are also ready to follow your goods through their journey after you have just attached a tag to them.
